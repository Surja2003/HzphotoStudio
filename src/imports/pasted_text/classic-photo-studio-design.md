"HZ photo Studio" located on Boring Road, Patna, Bihar.
The studio offers portrait photography, wedding photography, 
product photography, passport photos, photo printing, 
framing, and studio shoots.

This is the highest-budget prompt in the series — 
every section must feel editorial, immersive, and world-class.
Think Vogue portfolio meets local studio excellence.
